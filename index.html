What is this?

This is a 100% free poker game where you can set private tables for up to 10 of your friends. You can choose any room name you want and your name.
Whoever joins that pokergame can join the game. I hope you can enjoy pokernights with your friends! And if you enjoy the game and beers:

Instructions

1. Arrange with one or more of your friends when you want to play, pick a poker room name that should be unique and who is going to be the HOST (that person should tick "I am the game host")
2. Click on Start a new game
3. Once you all are in the videoconference, the HOST should just click Start
The host runs the server, he/she has to be present for the whole duration of the game and can take actions for other players in case they can't.
During the game or at the end of the round, click on your cards to show them.
If you bust, click on your name tag to rebuy.
